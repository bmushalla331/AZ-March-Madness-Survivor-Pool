Pool Rules
📋 Core Rules
  • No RepeatsOnce you pick a team, you cannot pick them again for the rest of the tournament. Previously used teams are removed from your selection list.
  • Only Active TeamsTeams that have already lost are removed from all selection lists automatically.
  • Win OutrightAll picks are based on the team winning their game — not against the spread.
  • Picks Lock at Tip-OffPicks for each round lock at the first scheduled tip-off of that round. No picks after the lock = automatic loss for that round.
  • Elimination — Any RoundAny single incorrect pick in any round eliminates you immediately. Admin can restore players who were eliminated in Rounds 1–4 only. Eliminations after Round 4 are permanent.
  • Re-Entry PenaltyRestored players must pick 4 teams if eliminated in R1/R2, or 5 teams if eliminated in R3/R4. Go perfect in the penalty round and you're back to the normal pick count.
  • Winner Take AllLast survivor wins the pool. Multiple survivors may agree to split.
🗓️ Entry Rounds (R1 & R2)
  • Round 1 — Thursday, Mar 20Every player picks 2 teams. No elimination risk — results carry forward.
  • Round 2 — Friday, Mar 21✅ Perfect R1? → Pick 2 teams.

    ❌ Any loss in R1? → Picks double: must pick 4 teams. No previously used teams. Still no elimination risk.
⚡ Carry-Forward Rule (R3+)
  • Perfect last round?Pick just 1 team next round. You've earned it.
  • Any loss last round?Required picks double — last round's count × 2.

    Examples: 1→2, 2→4, 4→8.
  • Elimination — any round.Any single loss in any round eliminates you immediately. Admin can restore eliminated players up to Round 4 only.

🔒 Round Lock Management
Locking a round prevents all new picks. Players with no picks for that round are automatically marked as a loss. Round 4+ losses result in elimination.
