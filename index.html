How It Works
Pool Rules
📋 Core Rules
  • Pick to Win OutrightAll picks are based on the team winning their game outright — not against the spread.
  • No RepeatsOnce you pick a team, you cannot pick them again for the rest of the tournament. Used teams are removed from your selection list automatically.
  • Only Active TeamsTeams that have been eliminated from the tournament are removed from the pick list automatically after each round.
  • Picks Lock at First Tip-OffPicks lock automatically when the first game of each round day begins. Once locked, no new picks can be submitted. Missing picks when the round locks are recorded as automatic losses.
  • Any Loss = EliminatedAny single incorrect pick in any round eliminates you immediately.
  • Admin Restore (R1–R4 only)Admin can restore an eliminated player, but only if they were eliminated in Rounds 1–4. Eliminations after Round 4 are permanent.
  • Re-Entry PenaltyRestored players must pick extra teams — 4 teams if eliminated in R1/R2, or 5 teams if eliminated in R3/R4. Go perfect in the penalty round and you return to normal pick counts the following round.
  • Winner Take AllLast survivor wins. Multiple survivors may agree to split the prize.
🗓️ R1 & R2 — Entry Rounds
  • Round 1 — Thu Mar 19Every player picks 2 teams. Any loss = eliminated. Admin can restore you for R2 with a penalty.
  • Round 2 — Fri Mar 20 ✅ Perfect R1 → pick 2 teams.

    🔄 Restored after R1 loss → pick 4 teams (penalty). Go clean = back to normal next round. Any loss = eliminated again.
⚡ R3+ — Carry-Forward Rule
  • Perfect last round?Pick just 1 team next round.
  • Any loss last round?Required picks double — previous round count × 2.

    Examples: 1→2 picks, 2→4 picks, 4→8 picks.
  • Any loss in any round = eliminated immediately.Admin restore available only up to Round 4.
